Good to know

Is this legitimate medical care?

Yes. RE GEN is the prescription arm of Hello Gorgeous Med Spa, a nurse-practitioner-directed medical practice. A licensed provider reviews your intake before anything is prescribed.

What does shipping cost?

A flat $30 ships your order discreetly to your door, anywhere we serve. Pricing for each medication is shown on its treatment page.

Are compounded medications FDA-approved?

Compounded medications are prepared by a licensed pharmacy for an individual patient and are not FDA-approved products. Your provider will review whether a compounded option is appropriate for you.

Do I need to come in to the clinic?

Most treatments can be started 100% online. Some therapies may require labs or an in-person visit in Oswego, IL — your provider will let you know.
