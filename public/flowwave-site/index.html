The technology

Cellular Response Technology

FlowWave™ FOCUS uses an electrohydraulic spark-gap system with a custom saline-filled head to send focused acoustic waves deep into tissue — targeting the source of discomfort, not just the surface.

Those waves travel through tissue at speeds above 3,500 mph, triggering biochemical reactions that support local blood flow, encourage the body's natural healing response, and help restore mobility — all without needles, medication, or recovery time. And it runs remarkably quiet.

Common questions

Good to know

Does shockwave therapy hurt?

Most clients describe it as a firm tapping sensation that's very tolerable. Your provider adjusts intensity to your comfort throughout the session.

How many sessions will I need?

It depends on the area and your goals. Many clients follow a short weekly course; your nurse practitioner will map out a plan at your free consultation.

Is there any downtime?

No. Sessions run about 3–10 minutes per area and you can return to your normal day right afterward.

Am I a candidate?

We screen every client first. Certain conditions — such as pregnancy, pacemakers, or metal implants in the treatment area — mean shockwave isn't appropriate. That's exactly what your free medical screening is for.

Is the men's treatment confidential?

Absolutely. Men's wellness treatments are provider-directed, private, and handled with full discretion.

Contraindications
Do not use if you have
  • Heart-rate regulator, cardiac pacemaker, or ECG device
  • Artificial bone
  • Silicosis
  • Intoxication or high fever
  • High-sensitivity mental illness or epilepsy
  • No independent awareness or inability to self-care
  • Metal in the body (including metal teeth), pregnancy, cachexia, or malignant tumor
  • Severe hypertension, or severe cardiovascular / cerebrovascular disease
  • Renal failure
  • Severe infection
Use only under professional guidance
  • During menstruation
  • Children
  • Hemorrhagic disease or trauma, inflammation, skin disease, or skin infection
  • Numbness, or no response to hot and cold
  • Immune-system deficiency, scar-formation history, or physical illness
  • Prior face-lifting, or any artificial / cosmetic implant in the body
