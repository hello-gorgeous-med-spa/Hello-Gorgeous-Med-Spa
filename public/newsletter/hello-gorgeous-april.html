Skincare Tips for Spring

Three simple shifts that make a big difference as the seasons change.

Switch to a Lighter Moisturizer

The heavy creams that carried your skin through winter can start clogging pores as humidity rises in spring. Look for gel-based or water-gel formulas with hyaluronic acid that hydrate without weight. Your skin will breathe easier — and your makeup will sit better, too.

Sunscreen Is Non-Negotiable (Yes, Still)

Every treatment we offer works harder when protected from UV exposure. Morpheus8, peels, CO₂, even Botox — sun damage can reverse your investment faster than anything else. SPF 30+ minimum, every single day. Mineral-based formulas are our top recommendation.

Don't Skip Your Vitamin Injections

B12, B-Complex, and MIC lipotropic injections are a fast, effective way to support energy, metabolism, and mood as your body adjusts to the season. Most clients notice a difference within 24–48 hours. Ask us about adding one to your next visit.
