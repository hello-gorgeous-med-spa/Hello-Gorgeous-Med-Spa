So What Is It, Really?

Tirzepatide is an FDA-approved peptide medication for weight management and type 2 diabetes. What makes it distinct is that it's a dual agonist — it activates two gut-hormone pathways at once (GLP-1 and GIP), where semaglutide works on one. Like semaglutide, it's a once-weekly injection, always prescription and provider-guided, and started low then stepped up.
