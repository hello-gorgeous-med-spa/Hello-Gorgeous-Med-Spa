Before anything from this list goes near a patient Most of these are research-use-only and several are FDA Category-2 flagged, meaning even a licensed 503A pharmacy may not be permitted to compound them for human use. Any decision about what is appropriate to offer must be made by Ryan Kent, FNP-BC as the prescribing clinician, with product sourced from a licensed pharmacy and your malpractice carrier informed. This sheet is a reference, not a green light.
