What to Expect

The Process

1

Let's Talk FirstYour Consultation

We'll start by sitting down together to talk about your goals, your lifestyle, and the look you're dreaming of. I'll review your skin type and health history, answer every question, and recommend the technique that will heal most beautifully for you — whether that's soft shading, crisp hair strokes, or a combination of both. No pressure, ever.

2

Finding Your Perfect ColorThe Color Test

Your pigment is chosen specifically for your skin tone, undertone, and the natural color of your hair and existing brows. I'll test shades against your skin so we land on a color that looks soft and natural as it heals — never harsh, never "too dark." You'll see and approve the color before we ever begin.

You approve the color first
3

Designed for Your FaceThe Mapping & Drawing

This is my favorite part. Using precise measuring points, I hand-draw and map your new brow shape directly onto your skin — tailored to your unique bone structure and facial symmetry. We'll look in the mirror together and adjust until the shape feels perfectly you. Nothing permanent happens until you say "yes, that's it."

Nothing begins until you love the shape
4

The Gentle PartYour Procedure

Once you've approved your shape and color, we begin. I use a gentle numbing process to keep you comfortable, and I'll talk you through each stage. Most clients are surprised by how relaxing it is — many even doze off! You're in caring, experienced hands the entire time.

5

Your Perfecting VisitThe Touch-Up

Your brows aren't truly "finished" on day one. About 6–8 weeks later, you'll come back for a complimentary perfecting touch-up, where we refine the shape, color, and density now that everything has healed. This visit is what takes your brows from beautiful to flawless.

Final results come at the touch-up

After Your Appointment

Healing & Aftercare

Healing is a journey of its own — here's the road map so nothing surprises you.

Days 1–2Bold
Brows look dark & bold. Totally normal! Gently blot as I'll show you. This is the most dramatic they'll ever look.
Days 3–5Darken
Color deepens, light flaking begins. They may feel a little tight or itchy — please don't pick! Let everything shed on its own.
Days 6–10Flaking
Flaking finishes. Brows may suddenly look light or patchy. Don't worry — the pigment is settling beneath the surface.
Wk 2Ghosting
The "ghosting" stage. Color may seem to nearly vanish. This is the part where everyone panics — and it's the part you can completely trust. It comes back.
Wk 4–6True Color
Your real, healed brows appear — softer and more natural than day one. This is your true result.

Please Do

  • Keep brows clean & dry as instructed
  • Gently blot the first day or two
  • Let all flakes fall on their own
  • Be patient through the healing stages
  • Text me with any questions — anytime

Please Don't

  • Pick, scratch, or peel the flakes
  • Get them wet or sweaty (about 10 days)
  • Wear makeup on the area while healing
  • Sun, pools, saunas, or tanning (2 weeks)
  • Use retinol or exfoliants on your brows

You're Never Doing This Alone

Healing has ups and downs, and that's completely normal. If anything ever worries you, reach out — I'd always rather hear from you and reassure you than have you wonder. Your peace of mind matters to me just as much as your beautiful brows.
