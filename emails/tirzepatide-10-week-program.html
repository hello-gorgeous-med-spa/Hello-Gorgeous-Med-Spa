Your weekly dose, made simple

We train you in person first — this is just your quick reference. Always follow the instructions from your provider.

1 Pick your site. Belly (2+ inches from the navel), back of the upper arm, or upper-outer thigh — rotate each week.
2 Prepare your dose. Wash hands, wipe the vial's stopper with alcohol, draw up past your dose, tap out air bubbles, then push back down to your exact prescribed amount.
3 Inject. Clean the area, pinch 1–2 inches of skin, insert at 90°, press the plunger until the dose is in, then hold a swab on the spot for a few minutes.
4 Dispose safely. Used needles go in a sharps or puncture-proof container — never household trash.
