# VIP Model Program — Pre & Post Treatment Protocols

Pre- and post-care protocols for all four VIP Model tiers. Clients receive these instructions before and after treatment.

---

## TIER 1 — MORPHEUS8 BURST (Collagen Rebuild)

**Full guide:** [Pre-Post Care: Morpheus8 Burst](/pre-post-care/morpheus8-burst)

### PRE-TREATMENT

**2 Weeks Before**
- Avoid sun exposure and tanning (including self-tanners)
- Stop retinoids (Retin-A, tretinoin, retinol)
- Discontinue AHAs, BHAs, glycolic acid
- Avoid waxing, threading, depilatory creams on treatment area
- Stay well-hydrated

**1 Week Before**
- Stop blood-thinning supplements if medically approved (fish oil, vitamin E, aspirin, ibuprofen)
- No chemical peels, laser, or microdermabrasion
- Avoid Botox or filler in treatment area
- Begin antiviral if prescribed (history of cold sores)
- No alcohol 48 hours before

**Day Of**
- Arrive with clean skin — no makeup, lotions, sunscreen
- Eat a normal meal
- Wear comfortable, loose clothing
- Topical numbing applied 20–30 minutes
- Treatment: 30–60 minutes

### POST-TREATMENT

**First 24 Hours**
- No makeup
- No touching with unwashed hands
- No sweating (exercise, saunas, hot tubs)
- Apply hyaluronic acid serum
- Sleep on back if face treated
- Cool compresses for swelling (not ice directly)

**Days 2–7**
- Gentle cleanser only
- Hyaluronic acid + gentle moisturizer
- SPF 30+ daily, reapply every 2 hours if outdoors
- No retinol, vitamin C, or actives for 7 days
- No exfoliating or picking
- Mineral makeup OK after 24–48 hours

**Weeks 2–4**
- Gradually reintroduce normal skincare
- Continue SPF 30+ daily
- Retinol may resume after 2 weeks
- Stay hydrated

**Results:** Day 1–3 redness; Day 3–5 peeling; Day 7 makeup-ready; Month 1–6 progressive tightening.

## TIER 2 — SOLARIA CO₂ LASER (Skin Resurfacing)

**Full guide:** [Pre-Post Care: Solaria CO₂](/pre-post-care/solaria-co2)

### ⚠️ ANTIVIRAL REQUIRED
If you have **any history of cold sores (HSV-1)**, you **MUST** take antiviral (Valtrex) starting **2 days before** and continuing **7–10 days after**. CO₂ can trigger severe outbreak. Notify provider for prescription.

### PRE-TREATMENT

**4 Weeks Before**
- Stop all retinoids
- Avoid prolonged sun; use SPF 30+ daily
- No tanning beds or self-tanners

**2 Weeks Before**
- Discontinue AHAs, BHAs, glycolic, vitamin C
- No waxing, threading, chemical peels
- Stop blood thinners if medically approved
- Avoid Botox/filler in treatment area
- Begin antiviral if prescribed

**Day Of**
- Completely clean skin — no makeup, lotions, sunscreen
- Eat a normal meal
- Arrange ride home (recommended for full face)
- Wear button-up shirt
- Numbing cream 30–45 minutes
- Treatment: 30–60 minutes

### POST-TREATMENT

**First 48 Hours**
- Soak and apply ointment (Aquaphor) every 2–4 hours
- Gently clean with diluted vinegar or prescribed cleanser
- **Keep skin moist at all times** — do NOT let it dry out
- No makeup
- Sleep elevated on 2 pillows
- Cool compresses for comfort
- Take prescribed pain medication
- Continue antiviral

**Days 3–7**
- Continue gentle cleansing and moisturizing
- **DO NOT pick, pull, or scrub** peeling skin
- Let dead skin shed naturally
- Switch to gentle moisturizer when peeling begins
- No sun, exercise, hot tubs, saunas, swimming
- No retinol or actives

**Weeks 2–4**
- New pink skin visible — healthy new tissue
- SPF 30+ CRITICAL — reapply every 2 hours outdoors
- Mineral makeup after provider approval
- No retinol until provider clears (usually 4 weeks)

**Results:** Day 1–3 intense redness/oozing; Day 3–5 peeling; Day 7–10 new skin visible; Month 1–6 collagen remodeling.

## TIER 3 — MORPHEUS8 + CO₂ COMBO (Total Skin Rebuild)

**Full guides:** [Morpheus8 Burst](/pre-post-care/morpheus8-burst) · [Solaria CO₂](/pre-post-care/solaria-co2)

Follow the **stricter** of the two protocols. CO₂ recovery is more intensive.

### PRE-TREATMENT
- Combine Morpheus8 + Solaria requirements
- **4 weeks before:** Stop retinoids
- **2 weeks before:** Stop actives, blood thinners; begin antiviral if cold sore history
- **Day of:** Clean skin, numbing, arrange ride for full-face combo

### POST-TREATMENT
- Plan for **5–7 days** social downtime (CO₂-driven)
- First 48 hours: Soak + ointment every 2–4 hours; keep skin moist
- Days 3–7: No picking; gentle cleansing; no sun, sweat, actives
- Weeks 2–4: SPF 30+ critical; mineral makeup when cleared
- Retinol: Hold until provider clears (typically 4 weeks)

## TIER 4 — THE TRIFECTA (Morpheus8 + CO₂ + Quantum RF)

**Full guides:** [Morpheus8 Burst](/pre-post-care/morpheus8-burst) · [Solaria CO₂](/pre-post-care/solaria-co2) · [Quantum RF](/pre-post-care/quantum-rf)

### QUANTUM RF ADDITIONS

**Pre-Treatment**
- **2 weeks before:** Avoid sun; stop smoking; purchase compression garment for body areas
- **1 week before:** Stop blood thinners; no alcohol 48 hours; arrange ride; plan 3–5 days reduced activity
- **Day of:** Bring compression garment for body; local anesthesia; treatment 45–90 minutes

**Post-Treatment**
- **First 48 hours:** Wear compression garment 24/7 (body); ice 20 on/20 off; no exercise
- **Days 3–14:** Continue compression 1–2 weeks; light walking OK; no heavy lifting 2 weeks
- **Weeks 2–6:** Resume activity gradually; follow up at 4–6 weeks

### COMBINED TRIFECTA PROTOCOL
- Follow Solaria pre/post for facial resurfacing
- Follow Quantum RF pre/post for body/contouring areas
- Follow Morpheus8 for RF microneedling component
- Treatments may be staged; provider will customize timeline
