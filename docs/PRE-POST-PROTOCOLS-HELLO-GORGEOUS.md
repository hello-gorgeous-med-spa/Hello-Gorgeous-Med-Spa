### 1. Botox / Neuromodulator Prep
**Category match:** botox  
**Send:** Email, 48 hours before

**1 Week Before**
- Stop blood thinners (if medically safe): Aspirin, Ibuprofen (Advil/Motrin), Fish Oil, Vitamin E
- Avoid Ginkgo Biloba, Garlic supplements, and Ginseng
- Continue any prescription blood thinners unless otherwise directed by your physician

**48–72 Hours Before**
- No alcohol (increases bruising risk)
- Stay hydrated — drink plenty of water
- Avoid strenuous exercise the day before

**Day Of**
- Clean skin — no makeup on treatment area
- Eat a light meal before your appointment
- Arrive 5–10 minutes early to fill out any forms

**What to Expect**
- Treatment: 10–15 minutes
- Small bumps at injection sites (temporary)
- Results visible in 3–14 days
- **No lying down for 4 hours after treatment**

**Contact:** (630) 636-6193 | hello@hellogorgeousmedspa.com

### 2. Dermal Filler Prep
**Category match:** fillers  
**Send:** Email, 72 hours before

**1 Week Before**
- Stop blood thinners (if medically safe): Aspirin, Ibuprofen, Fish Oil, Vitamin E, Ginkgo Biloba
- Avoid alcohol 48–72 hours before
- Start Arnica supplements if desired (helps reduce bruising)

**48 Hours Before**
- No dental work 2 weeks before/after lip fillers
- Avoid excessive sun exposure
- If history of cold sores, let us know for prophylactic medication

**Day Of**
- No makeup on treatment area
- Eat normally; come well-hydrated
- Plan for potential swelling (especially lip filler)

**What to Expect**
- Treatment: 30–60 minutes depending on areas
- Numbing cream applied
- Some swelling and bruising normal (peaks 48–72 hours)
- Results immediate but settle in 2 weeks

**After Your Appointment**
- No strenuous exercise 24–48 hours
- Avoid extreme heat (sauna, hot yoga) 48 hours
- Sleep elevated the first night to reduce swelling

**Contact:** (630) 636-6193

### 3. GLP-1 / Weight Loss Visit Prep

### 4. HydraFacial / Facial Prep
**Category match:** facials  
**Send:** Email, 24 hours before

**3–5 Days Before**
- Stop retinoids (Retin-A, Tretinoin, Retinol) 3–5 days before
- No chemical peels or laser 2 weeks prior
- Avoid excessive sun

**24 Hours Before**
- No waxing or threading
- No exfoliating products
- Stay hydrated

**Day Of**
- Arrive with clean skin — no makeup, sunscreen, or skincare
- Mention any skin sensitivities or new products

**What to Expect**
- Treatment: 45–60 minutes
- Cleansing, exfoliation, extraction, hydration
- Immediate glow; no downtime

**After**
- Apply sunscreen; avoid heavy makeup for a few hours

**Contact:** (630) 636-6193

### 5. Microneedling / PRP Prep
**Category match:** prp  
**Send:** Email, 72 hours before

**1 Week Before**
- Stop retinoids (Retin-A, Tretinoin) 5–7 days before
- Stop Accutane at least 6 months before
- Avoid blood thinners (Aspirin, Ibuprofen, Fish Oil)

**48–72 Hours Before**
- No chemical peels, laser, or waxing
- Avoid excessive sun; no alcohol

**Day Of**
- Arrive with clean skin — no makeup
- Eat a normal meal (especially for PRP — we draw blood)
- Well-hydrated (important for PRP blood draw)

**What to Expect**
- Numbing cream 20–30 minutes
- PRP: small blood draw from arm
- Treatment: 45–90 minutes total
- Mild redness (sunburn-like)

**After Treatment**
- Redness 24–72 hours normal
- No makeup 24 hours
- No sweating/exercise 24–48 hours
- Avoid sun; use SPF diligently

**Contact:** (630) 636-6193

### 7. Lash Extensions Prep
**Category match:** lash  
**Send:** SMS, 24 hours before

**Before**
- Clean lashes — no mascara or eye makeup
- Remove contacts (glasses recommended)
- Avoid caffeine if possible

**Day Of**
- Shower beforehand (no water on lashes 24 hours after)
- No eye makeup
- Comfortable clothing; lying down 1–2+ hours

**What to Expect**
- Full set: 2–2.5 hours; Fill: 45–90 minutes
- Eyes closed; painless and relaxing

**Aftercare Preview**
- No water on lashes 24 hours
- No oil-based products near eyes
- Brush daily with spoolie
- Fills every 2–3 weeks

## POST / AFTERCARE

**Where it lives**
- **Per visit:** Charting → Post-care and Before/after notes (free text per chart note).
- **Auto-send:** Admin → Settings → Aftercare Instructions. When an appointment is marked **completed**, the system can send an aftercare template (email/SMS) by service. Templates are editable; you can seed defaults from that page (“Load Default Templates”) if the aftercare seed API is deployed.

**Standard post-care by category** (from your pre protocols and common practice):

| Service              | Key post-care |
|----------------------|----------------|
| **Botox**            | No lying down 4 hours; no rubbing; avoid heat/sauna 24 hrs; no strenuous exercise 24 hrs. |
| **Filler**           | No strenuous exercise 24–48 hrs; no extreme heat 48 hrs; sleep elevated first night; avoid dental 2 weeks (lips). |
| **Microneedling/PRP**| No makeup 24 hrs; no sweating/exercise 24–48 hrs; sun avoidance + SPF; redness 24–72 hrs normal. |
| **HydraFacial**      | Sunscreen; avoid heavy makeup few hours. |
| **IV Therapy**       | Hydrate; normal activities. |
| **Lash extensions**  | No water on lashes 24 hrs; no oil near eyes; brush with spoolie; fills 2–3 weeks. |
