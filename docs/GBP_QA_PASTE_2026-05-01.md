1. Open **Google Maps** on your phone (signed in as the owner)
2. Tap your profile photo (top right) -> **Your Business profile**
3. Tap **Hello Gorgeous Med Spa** -> the **Q&A** tab
4. Tap **Ask a question**, paste the **question**, post
5. Tap the question you just posted -> **Answer**, paste the **answer**, post
6. Repeat for each entry below

(Owner-posted Q&A is allowed by Google policy as long as the
question is something a customer might genuinely ask.)
