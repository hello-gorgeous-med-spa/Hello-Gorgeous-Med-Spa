# Hello Gorgeous — 5 Agents Runbook

**You're stretched thin (work + med school). You don't have capital for a full-time admin. Your sister runs BootsAgentAI with 5 agents daily — this is the same idea for Hello Gorgeous: five clear "agents" (workflows) that keep your name out there using what already works.**

---

## Use what works; don't wait on what doesn't

| Channel | Status | Use it for |
|--------|--------|------------|
| **Square Marketing** | ✅ Works | Email campaigns to ~2,700 contacts. Your main outbound until Telnyx is fixed. |
| **Facebook** | ✅ Up | Posts, offers, DMs, events. Post 2–4x/week. |
| **Instagram** | ✅ Up | Posts, stories, reels, DMs. Same content as FB when it fits. |
| **Google Business** | ✅ Up | Posts (offers/updates) 1–2x/week. Use Admin → Marketing → Google Post Campaigns for copy. |
| **Website** | ✅ Live | Booking, chat, VIP popup, content. No extra work — just keep it current. |
| **Telnyx SMS** | ⚠️ Compliance issues | Pause outbound marketing SMS until 10DLC/brand is approved. Use for inbound/replies only if you want. Don't let it block you. |

**Bottom line:** Get your name out with **Square email + Facebook + Instagram + Google posts**. Add SMS back when Telnyx passes compliance.

---

## The 5 agents (what an admin would do — automated or templated)

### Agent 1 — Social / Presence  
**Job:** Keep Hello Gorgeous visible on FB, IG, and Google.

- **Google:** 1–2 posts per week. Use **Admin → Marketing → Google Post Campaigns** (copy-paste from the list). Rotate: Glow Event, Botox $10/unit, Lip Filler, Weight Loss, VIP waitlist.
- **Facebook:** 2–4 posts per week. Same offers + behind-the-scenes, client love, Morpheus8 & Solaria now booking (Quantum RF coming soon). Use Square or Meta Business Suite to schedule if you like.
- **Instagram:** Match FB when it fits; add stories (quick tips, "book now," Glow Event flyer).

**Weekly (15 min):** Pick 2 Google post campaigns, 2–3 FB/IG ideas from the list below. Paste or schedule. Done.

---

### Agent 2 — Inbox / Leads  
**Job:** Respond to DMs, comments, and contact form so no lead goes cold.

- **Triage:** Check FB/IG DMs and contact form 1x/day (or have a VA do it).
- **Templates** (save in Notes or a doc; paste and personalize):
  - *"Hi! Thanks for reaching out. We'd love to help. What are you most interested in — injectables, weight loss, or something else? You can also book a free consult here: [book link]."*
  - *"So glad you're interested! Our Glow Event is running — Botox $10/unit, lip filler $499, free vitamin injection for new clients. Book here [link] and mention Glow Event."*
- **Contact form:** Site already sends to your email; reply within 24 hours with the same tone + book link.

**Daily (5 min):** Scan DMs and contact form; send 1–2 template replies. Done.

---

### Agent 3 — Reviews  
**Job:** Get more Google (and FB) reviews and respond to every one.

- **Ask for reviews:** After every visit, send an email (Square or your system) with: *"If you loved your visit, we'd love a Google review — it helps others find us: [review link]."*  
  Review link: `https://g.page/r/CYQOWmT_HcwQEBM/review`
- **Respond to every review** (Google Business Profile → Reviews). Short, personal, thank them. Template: *"Thank you so much! We're so glad you had a great experience. Can't wait to see you again."*

**Weekly (10 min):** Send 1 "please review" email to recent clients (or automate post-visit). Check Google (and FB) for new reviews; respond. Done.

---

### Agent 4 — Fill the calendar  
**Job:** Use email to remind people you exist and drive bookings.

- **Square Marketing:** You have ~2,700 contacts. Run 1 email per week:
  - Week 1: Glow Event (Botox $10/unit, lip filler $499, free vitamin, weight loss).
  - Week 2: "We have openings this week" + book link.
  - Week 3: First-time client offer (free vitamin injection when you book).
  - Week 4: Morpheus8 & Solaria now booking — Quantum RF coming soon.
  - Repeat or mix.
- **Subject lines:** Keep them short. "Your Glow Event invite 💉" "Openings this week at Hello Gorgeous" "Free vitamin injection when you book"
- **CTA:** One button: Book now → `https://www.hellogorgeousmedspa.com/book`

**Weekly (20 min):** Draft one email in Square (or use a template), hit send. Done.

---

### Agent 5 — Promo / Offers  
**Job:** One clear offer at a time, everywhere.

- **Right now:** Glow Event. Same message on Google post, FB, IG, Square email: FREE vitamin injection, Botox $10/unit, lip filler $499, weight loss from $600. Mention "Glow Event" when you book.
- **Next:** When Glow Event winds down, switch to "Botox $10/unit" or "First-time client: free vitamin injection" as the hero. Keep one offer; repeat it everywhere.
- **Links:** Book = `https://www.hellogorgeousmedspa.com/book`  
  Glow Event page = `https://www.hellogorgeousmedspa.com/glow-event`  
  VIP waitlist = `https://www.hellogorgeousmedspa.com/vip-skin-tightening`

**Weekly (5 min):** Decide the "offer of the week." Make sure it's on Google post, one FB/IG post, and in the Square email. Done.

## Weekly checklist (one page)

Print or keep this open. Do it once a week (or split across 2–3 days).

| Agent | Task | Where | Time |
|-------|------|--------|------|
| 1. Social | Post 1–2 Google posts | [Google Business](https://business.google.com) → Posts | 10 min |
| 1. Social | Post 2–4x FB/IG | Facebook/Instagram or Meta Business Suite | 15 min |
| 2. Inbox | Reply to DMs + contact form | FB/IG app + email | 5 min/day |
| 3. Reviews | Ask 5–10 recent clients for review (email) | Square or your list | 10 min |
| 3. Reviews | Respond to any new Google/FB reviews | Google Business → Reviews | 5 min |
| 4. Calendar | Send 1 Square email campaign | [Square Marketing](https://squareup.com/dashboard/marketing) | 20 min |
| 5. Promo | Confirm "offer of the week" is everywhere | Google + FB/IG + email | 5 min |

**Total:** ~1 hour per week of focused "agent" work. The rest is templates and systems.

## Telnyx — what to do

- **Option A:** Work with Telnyx support to get 10DLC/campaign registration approved so you can send marketing SMS again. (Worth doing when you have 30 min to spare.)
- **Option B:** Pause outbound SMS entirely. Use **Square for email only** (or Square's own SMS if they offer it and it's compliant). You're not "missing the boat" — you're on the boat with email + social + Google. Add SMS when it's unblocked.
- **Option C:** Use Telnyx for **inbound only** (calls/texts to your number) and don't send bulk SMS until compliant.

Don't let Telnyx hold your marketing hostage. Your $500k in equipment gets seen through **consistent posts, emails, and offers** — and that can all run on Square + social + Google today.
