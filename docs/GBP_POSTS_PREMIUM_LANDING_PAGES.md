# Google Business Profile posts — premium landing pages

Paste-ready copy for the three flagship SEO pages. Post one per week per
page (Google de-emphasizes posts older than ~7 days for ranking signal,
so a steady cadence beats one-time bursts).

## How to post (5-min process)

1. Sign in at [business.google.com](https://business.google.com) → pick
   the Hello Gorgeous Med Spa profile.
2. Left sidebar → **Add update** → choose **What's New**, **Offer**, or
   **Event**.
3. Paste the headline + body below, attach the recommended image, set
   the **Add a button** to "Learn more" with the URL shown.
4. Hit **Post**. It's live within minutes and crawled within hours.

> Image specs: 1200x900 (4:3) or 1024x576 (16:9), JPG or PNG, <5 MB.
> Use the Hello Gorgeous brand pink (#E6007E) accent + black borders to
> stay on-template.
