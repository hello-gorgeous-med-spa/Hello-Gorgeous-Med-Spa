## Claim Links & Steps

### Yelp for Business

- **Claim:** https://biz.yelp.com/
- Search for "Hello Gorgeous Med Spa" or add new business
- Verify NAP matches exactly
- Add services, hours, photos
- Add booking link: hellogorgeousmedspa.com/book

### Healthgrades

- **Add practice:** https://www.healthgrades.com/group-practices/add-your-practice
- Or search existing listing and claim
- NAP must match
- Add provider(s): Danielle (FNP), Ryan (owner)

### Apple Maps (Maps Connect)

- **Claim:** https://mapsconnect.apple.com/
- Sign in with Apple ID
- Add or claim "Hello Gorgeous Med Spa"
- NAP, hours, website, phone
- Categories: Medical Spa, Beauty Salon (secondary)

### Bing Places for Business

- **Claim:** https://www.bingplaces.com/
- Add or claim listing
- NAP, hours, website, photos
- Syncs with other Microsoft properties

### Facebook

- Create Page or claim existing: https://www.facebook.com/business/pages
- NAP in About section
- Add services, booking link
- Connect to Instagram if applicable
