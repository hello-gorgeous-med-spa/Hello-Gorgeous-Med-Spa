# 12-Week Marketing Playbook — Hello Gorgeous Med Spa

**Goal:** Grow visibility, bookings, and word-of-mouth **without** a big ad budget — using Google, your website, your client list, and local relationships.

**Principles:** One **monthly theme** repeated everywhere (GBP, SMS, email, one social channel). **Proof** (reviews, before/after with consent) beats polish. **Speed** answering calls/DMs is part of marketing.

**Quick links (bookmark these):**

| Resource | URL |
|----------|-----|
| Book | https://www.hellogorgeousmedspa.com/book |
| Financing | https://www.hellogorgeousmedspa.com/financing |
| Blog | https://www.hellogorgeousmedspa.com/blog |
| Google review (use in texts/cards) | See your GBP “Get more reviews” link |
| GBP posts (copy ideas) | [GOOGLE_POST_CAMPAIGNS.md](./GOOGLE_POST_CAMPAIGNS.md) |

**Weekly rhythm (about 2–4 hours total):** ~30–45 min Monday (plan + GBP), ~15 min mid-week (SMS or email), ~30 min Friday (review requests + one content piece).

---

## Week 1 — Foundation

**Theme:** Your house is in order before you invite more people in.

| Action | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Google Business Profile | Confirm **hours, phone, address, website** match your door and site. Add **10+ recent photos** (team, rooms, devices, reception — not only stock). |
| Services | In GBP, ensure **service list** includes high-intent terms people search (Botox, fillers, weight loss, Morpheus8, laser, etc.), not only “med spa.” |
| Review system | Create a **one-sentence script** for front desk: *“If you’re happy today, we’d love a Google review — I can text you the link.”* Print or save a **QR** to your review URL if you use one. |
| List | Export or note **active client emails/phones** (where you’re allowed to market). Segment later: “seen in last 90 days” vs “inactive 6+ months.” |

---

## Week 2 — Google + proof

**Theme:** Be the obvious choice when someone searches Oswego / Naperville area.

| Action | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| GBP post | Post **1 Update** linking to **one blog article** or **/financing** — use [GOOGLE_POST_CAMPAIGNS.md](./GOOGLE_POST_CAMPAIGNS.md). |
| Q&A | In GBP, add **3–5 questions you hear daily** (“Do you offer Botox?” “Financing?”) with short answers. |
| Reviews | **Ask 5 happy clients** this week (in person + text link). Respond to **every** review within 48 hours. |

---

## Week 3 — One story, many channels

**Theme:** Repeat **one** message so it sticks.

| Action | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Pick a theme | Examples: **skin tightening** (Morpheus8 / Quantum), **weight loss**, **injectables**, or **financing**. |
| Website | Ensure **one** service page or blog post is the “hero” you’ll push for 2 weeks — share link internally. |
| SMS or email | **One** send: education + soft CTA to book (not 10 offers). Link **financing** if price is a barrier. |
| Social | **3 short posts or Reels** (same theme): device clip, Danielle voice-over, or client quote (with consent). |

---

## Week 4 — Reactivation

**Theme:** Cheaper to bring someone back than to buy a stranger.

| Action | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Inactive list | Message **clients 6–12 months quiet**: “We miss you — here’s one reason to come back” + **small add-on** or consult, not a huge discount. |
| GBP | **Offer** post with clear end date OR link to **Glow event** / book if you run events. |
| Track | Note **bookings** from that message (even a tally sheet) so you know what to repeat. |

---

## Week 5 — Local partnership (one)

**Theme:** Borrow someone else’s audience.

| Action | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Partner outreach | **One** local business: salon, gym, bridal shop, dentist, chiropractor. Offer **reciprocal referral** or a **single lunch-and-learn** for their staff (15 min + cards). |
| Collateral | **10 cards** or a simple half-page: services + **book URL** + **financing URL**. |

---

## Week 6 — Social proof push

**Theme:** Before/after and outcomes beat ad copy.

| Action | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Media | Schedule **2 photo sessions** (with **signed consent**) for before/after or testimonial video. |
| Site / GBP | Upload **2 new images** to GBP; add **one** proof block to site or blog if not there. |
| Reviews | Another **push for 5 reviews**; thank reviewers by name when you respond. |

---

## Week 7 — Double down on what worked

**Theme:** Data from Weeks 1–6 beats guessing.

| Action | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Review | What drove **calls or bookings**? (GBP post, SMS, partner, one service page?) **Do more of that.** |
| Cut | Stop anything that took time with **zero** signal (e.g. a post type nobody engaged with). |
| GBP | **2 posts** this week if you slacked earlier — consistency matters. |

---

## Week 8 — Education week

**Theme:** Teach = trust; trust = consults.

| Action | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Content | Publish or **repurpose** one blog piece (or short video) answering **“Is X worth it?”** or **“What to expect first visit.”** |
| Email/SMS | Teaser + link to that article; CTA = **book consult**. |
| GBP | **Update** post linking to the article (helps SEO + trust). |

---

## Week 9 — Finishing Q3 strong (or mid-year push)

**Theme:** Remove money friction without racing to the bottom.

| Action | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Financing | Mention **CareCredit + Cherry** in consult, **SMS**, and **GBP** once ([Campaign 11](./GOOGLE_POST_CAMPAIGNS.md)). |
| Offer | **One** clear package or event — not ten small discounts. |
| Team | Everyone uses the **same** financing one-liner so clients hear consistency. |

---

## Week 10 — Community visibility (micro)

**Theme:** Be seen locally without a billboard.

| Action | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Event | **One** of: small **open house**, **school/chamber table**, **charity raffle** donation with branded card + book QR. |
| PR | **Nextdoor** or **local Facebook group** — one helpful post (rules permitting), not a spam blast. |
| Photos | Post **event photos** to GBP and social. |

---

## Week 11 — Speed + experience

**Theme:** Operations are marketing.

| Action | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Response time | Track: **calls/DMs answered** within business hours. Fix voicemail message if outdated. |
| No-shows | Tighten **reminder** flow (text day before). |
| Surprise | **One** small “wow” — handwritten thank-you for high-value visits or referrals (budget: stamps + time). |

---

## Week 12 — Plan the next quarter

**Theme:** Repeat what works; level up one channel.

| Action | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Scorecard | **Reviews gained**, **GBP posts**, **SMS/email sends**, **partner meetings**, **bookings** tied to campaigns. |
| Next quarter | Pick **one channel** to deepen (e.g. **monthly email** + **weekly GBP**) and **one experiment** (e.g. **$10/day local search** for 2 weeks only on high-intent keywords — optional). |
| Calendar | Block **recurring** Monday 30 min “marketing” on your calendar so Weeks 1–12 become a **loop**, not a one-off. |

---

## Optional: tiny paid test (only after Weeks 1–8)

If you have **any** budget: **Google Local / Search** in your radius on **high-intent** terms, or **retarget** people who visited **/book** or **/services** — not broad “awareness.” Cap spend; measure booked consults, not likes.
