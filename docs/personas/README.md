# Patient Personas — Marketing

**Purpose:** Define who you serve so content hits the right audience. Use for blog, social, email.

**Not the same as:** Mascot personas (Peppi, Beau-Tox) — those are for chat. These are marketing personas.

---

## Personas to Create

1. **Botox First-Timer** — 35–50, considering injectables for first time
2. **Weight Loss Seeker** — 40–60, interested in GLP-1, tried diets
3. **Laser / Body Contouring** — 35–55, wants skin tightening, body contouring, or laser resurfacing

---

## Template (per persona)

- **Name:** (e.g. "Botox Beth")
- **Demographics:** Age range, location, income
- **Pain points:** What they're worried about
- **Where they search:** Google, social, referrals
- **What they need:** Education, reassurance, proof, pricing
- **Content that works:** Topics, tone, CTAs
