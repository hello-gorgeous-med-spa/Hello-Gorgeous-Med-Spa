## Rules for All New Content

1. **Blogs:** Include "Oswego, IL" or "in Oswego" in the **title** and **first 100 words**. Add surrounding cities (Naperville, Aurora, Plainfield, Fox Valley) where natural.
2. **Meta titles & descriptions:** Include at least one: Oswego, IL | Naperville | Aurora | Plainfield
3. **Service pages:** Hero titles should end with "in Oswego, IL" (e.g., "Botox in Oswego, IL")
4. **Testimonials / quotes:** Use client locations: "Oswego, IL", "Naperville, IL", "Aurora, IL"
5. **Social posts:** Include location: "Oswego, IL — serving Naperville, Aurora, Plainfield"

---

## Never Use

- Phoenix, Phoenix Valley (Arizona — not our location)
- Any city outside the Chicagoland / Fox Valley / Kendall / Kane area unless explicitly comparing or contrasting
