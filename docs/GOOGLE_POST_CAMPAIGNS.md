# Google Business Profile — Post Campaigns

Use these ready-made campaigns to post on your **Google Business Profile** so they show up when people search for you or "med spa Oswego IL". Post 1–2 times per week for best visibility.

---

## Where to post

1. Go to [Google Business Profile](https://business.google.com) and sign in.
2. Select **Hello Gorgeous Med Spa**.
3. In the left menu click **Posts** (or **Updates**).
4. Click **Create post** and choose type: **Offer**, **Update**, **Event**, or **Product**.

**Tips:**  
- **Offer** = promo with optional coupon code, end date, and “Book” button.  
- **Update** = general news or tip; great for education.  
- **Event** = one-time (e.g. Glow-Up Event).  
- Use the **Button** to link to your booking page or a specific URL below.

**Booking URL (use for most CTAs):**  
`https://www.hellogorgeousmedspa.com/book`
