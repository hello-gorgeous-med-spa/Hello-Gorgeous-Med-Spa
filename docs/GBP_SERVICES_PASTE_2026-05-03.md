# Google Business Profile — Services to add (paste manually)

Curated 24-item GBP "Services" list for Hello Gorgeous Med Spa. This is the
right altitude for GBP local-SEO matching: broad service categories with
short SEO-tuned descriptions — not the 130+ SKU-level items in Square.

Competitive context: HER Aesthetics (Oswego) lists ~23 categories. This list
matches their breadth and beats them on four category lanes HER doesn't
compete in:
  - Advanced Morpheus8 (Burst + Deep / Body — HER only has standard)
  - Premium laser (Solaria CO2 fractional resurfacing)
  - Non-surgical body contouring (Quantum RF Lipo)
  - Regenerative depth (PRP, Exosomes, AnteAGE stem cell, NAD+)

## How to add each one (desktop, fastest)

1. Open business.google.com -> sign in as the owner of Hello Gorgeous Med Spa
2. Edit profile -> **Services**
3. For each item below: **Add service** -> pick a category (or "Add custom service")
4. Paste the **Service name**, then paste the **Description** (300-char max)
5. Save and move on. ~30-45 minutes total for all 24.

Notes:
- Descriptions are ASCII-clean (no smart quotes / em dashes) so they paste
  without GBP's content validator throwing a fit.
- All descriptions stay under 280 characters with room for tweaks.
- Where GBP offers a structured category that matches (e.g. "Botox treatment",
  "Dermal filler", "Laser hair removal"), pick it. Use **Add custom service**
  for the rest.
