## 4. Your Rights Under HIPAA

### Right to Access
- Request copies of your records
- Electronic copies available
- Response within 30 days
- Reasonable fees may apply

### Right to Amend
- Request changes to records
- We may deny in writing
- Your request becomes part of record

### Right to Accounting
- List of disclosures made
- For 6 years prior to request
- Excludes treatment, payment, operations

### Right to Restrict
- Request limits on use/disclosure
- We are not required to agree
- If we agree, we are bound

### Right to Confidential Communication
- Request alternative contact methods
- Request specific locations
- We will accommodate reasonable requests

### Right to Paper Copy
- Receive paper copy of this notice
- Available at any time

## 7. SMS and Mobile Messaging

When you provide your mobile number and opt in to receive text messages, we may send you appointment reminders, treatment updates, and promotional offers via SMS. Message and data rates may apply.

**Your mobile information will not be sold or shared with third parties for promotional or marketing purposes.**

We use your phone number only to communicate with you about your appointments and, if you opt in, to send occasional promotional offers from Hello Gorgeous Med Spa. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message.
