Dear Ms. Gulamhusein and SMooth Solutions Medspa LLC:

I am writing on behalf of **Hello Gorgeous Med Spa** and its owners, Danielle Alcala-Glazier and Ryan Kent, RN, regarding false and misleading statements published on your commercial website, smoothsolutionsmedspa.com, that mischaracterize our business and our services.

Between November 14, 2025 and December 22, 2025, you published eight (8) articles on your website that contain verifiably false statements about Hello Gorgeous Med Spa. These articles are structured as "consumer guides" ranking med spas in Oswego, Illinois, but they are published on your own commercial website and consistently rank your business first while misrepresenting ours. Seven of the eight articles were published on the same date (November 14, 2025), indicating a coordinated campaign.

## False and Misleading Statements Requiring Immediate Correction

The following statements are **false and defamatory** and must be corrected or removed:

### 1. False claim that Hello Gorgeous "does not provide injectables"

In your article "Best Medspa in Oswego, Illinois," you state:

> "Because Hello Gorgeous is not a medical clinic, it does not provide injectables or body contouring; instead, it specializes in facial treatments and lash artistry."

**This is false.** Hello Gorgeous is a licensed medical spa that provides Botox, Dysport, Jeuveau, and dermal filler injections daily, performed by licensed medical professionals. This information is publicly available on our website, Fresha booking page, and Google Business Profile.

### 2. False claim that Hello Gorgeous "doesn't provide injectables or laser resurfacing"

In your article "Best Facial Treatments in Oswego, Illinois," you state:

> "Although Hello Gorgeous doesn't provide injectables or laser resurfacing, its high-quality facials and cosmetic services make it an essential part of Oswego's beauty landscape."

**This is false.** Hello Gorgeous provides injectables and operates the Solaria CO₂ fractional laser by InMode for skin resurfacing.

### 3. Misleading characterization as "boutique" focused on "brow and lash artistry"

Multiple articles characterize Hello Gorgeous as a "boutique" focused on "brow and lash artistry" or "cosmetic artistry." This omits the majority of our services, including:

- Botox, Dysport, Jeuveau, and dermal fillers  
- GLP-1 weight loss programs (semaglutide and tirzepatide)  
- Bioidentical hormone replacement therapy  
- Peptide therapy and PRP treatments  
- Morpheus8 with Burst technology, Quantum RF Lipo, Solaria CO₂ laser  
- Men's health services  

These omissions create a false impression that Hello Gorgeous is a beauty salon rather than a full-service medical spa.

### 4. False claim that Hello Gorgeous is "not a high-powered laser clinic"

In your article "Laser Resurfacing in Oswego, IL," you state:

> "While not a high-powered laser clinic, it provides personalized care and solid foundational skin health services that can complement future resurfacing plans if clients choose to pursue more intensive options down the line."

**This is false.** Hello Gorgeous operates the Solaria CO₂ fractional laser by InMode, one of the most advanced skin resurfacing systems available. The suggestion that clients should seek "more intensive options" elsewhere directs business away from Hello Gorgeous toward your company.

## Demand

We demand that you:

1. **Immediately cease** publishing, maintaining, or distributing any content that contains false or misleading statements about Hello Gorgeous Med Spa.

2. **Within fourteen (14) days** of the date of this letter:
   - Remove or substantially correct all eight (8) articles identified above so that they no longer contain false statements of fact about Hello Gorgeous; or
   - Remove all references to Hello Gorgeous from these articles if you are unable to accurately describe our services.

3. **Preserve** all documents, communications, and records relating to the creation, publication, and promotion of these articles.
