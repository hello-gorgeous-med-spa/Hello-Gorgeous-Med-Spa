# GBP Visibility Fixes — Why You're Not Showing for "Near Me"

**Problem:** Hello Gorgeous doesn't appear for "Morpheus8 near me," "Botox near me," or even "med spa Oswego" — while 4+ Oswego competitors do. Service area has been set for years.

**Based on your current GBP settings, here are the likely culprits and fixes.**

---

## Fix #1: Add Individual Services (Critical)

Google matches "Botox near me" and "Morpheus8 near me" to businesses that have those **exact services** listed in the **Services** section — not just in your description.

**Check:** In GBP → Edit profile → **Services** — do you have individual items like "Botox," "Morpheus8," "Laser hair removal," etc.?

The "Menu or services link" (Fresha) is separate — Google also wants the services listed as individual items in the profile.

---

## Fix #2: Change "Appointment not required" → "Appointment required"

You currently have **Appointment not required**. For a med spa, that's inaccurate and can hurt relevance.

**Fix:** GBP → Edit profile → **Service options** → Change to **"Appointment required"** or **"Appointments recommended"**

---

## Fix #3: Consider Removing Service Area (Test)

You have both a **physical address** (74 W Washington) and a **service area** (Aurora, Naperville, etc.). Some local SEO experts say that for a **storefront business** where clients come to you, adding a service area can dilute your map visibility — Google may treat you more like a "service-area business" and show you less often for map searches.

**Test:** Remove the service area temporarily (or reduce to just Oswego). See if visibility improves in 1–2 weeks. If it does, the service area may have been hurting. If not, add it back.

---

## Fix #4: Strengthen Description — Remove "day spa"

Your description says "your go-to one-stop **day spa**." "Day spa" signals something different from "medical spa" and can dilute relevance for medical/aesthetic searches.

**Fix:** Replace "day spa" with "medical spa" — e.g., "Hello Gorgeous centrally located in downtown Oswego is your go-to one-stop **medical spa**."

---

## Fix #5: Secondary Categories — Order Matters

You have: Medical spa (primary), Facial spa, Medical clinic, Skin care clinic, Weight loss service, Women's health clinic.

"Facial spa" as first secondary might compete with "Medical spa" for injectables/laser searches. Consider reordering so **Skin care clinic** and **Weight loss service** are higher — they match "Morpheus8" and "Botox" better than "Facial spa."

---

## Fix #6: Reviews (Prominence)

If competitors showing have 20–50+ reviews and you have fewer, that's a major factor. Google uses review count and recency as ranking signals.

**Action:** 3–5 new reviews per week. Use hellogorgeousmedspa.com/review and QR code at checkout.

---

## Priority Order

1. **Add/verify Services** — Individual items for Botox, Morpheus8, etc. (biggest potential impact)
2. **Appointment required** — Fix the attribute
3. **Description** — Change "day spa" to "medical spa"
4. **Test removing service area** — If nothing else moves the needle
5. **Reviews** — Ongoing
