# Aurora "Near Me" Visibility — Why You're Not Showing & How to Fix

**Problem:** When someone in Aurora (e.g., 1902 Conway Lane) searches "Morpheus8 near me" or "Botox near me," competitors in Aurora, Naperville, Plainfield show up — but Hello Gorgeous (Oswego) does not.

**Root cause:** Google Maps "near me" prioritizes (1) proximity, (2) GBP relevance, (3) prominence. Aurora searchers see Aurora businesses first. Oswego is ~10 miles away, so you're at a proximity disadvantage unless your GBP tells Google you serve Aurora.

---

## Fix #1: Add "Areas Served" in GBP (Most Important)

In Google Business Profile:

1. Go to **Profile** → **Edit profile** → **Service area** (or **Areas served**)
2. Add these cities you serve:
   - **Aurora**
   - **Naperville**
   - **Plainfield**
   - **Montgomery**
   - **Yorkville**
   - **Oswego**

This tells Google: "We serve clients from Aurora." Without it, Google may assume you only serve Oswego and deprioritize you for Aurora-based "near me" searches.

**How to add:** In GBP dashboard → Info → Add/edit "Service area" or "Areas served" (wording varies by interface). Add each city by name.

---

## Fix #2: Verify Services Are in GBP

Ensure these are explicitly listed in your **Services** section:

| Service | Exact wording to add |
|---------|----------------------|
| Morpheus8 | Morpheus8 (RF microneedling) |
| Morpheus8 Burst | Morpheus8 Burst (deep RF microneedling) |
| Botox | Botox |
| Dysport | Dysport |
| Jeuveau | Jeuveau |
| Dermal fillers | Dermal fillers |

If "Morpheus8" and "Botox" aren't in your services list, Google may not match you for those searches.

## Fix #3: Primary Category

- **Primary:** Medical Spa
- **Secondary (if allowed):** Skin care clinic, Laser hair removal service

"Medical Spa" is correct for injectables and advanced treatments. Don't change it.

---

## Fix #4: Reviews (Prominence)

Competitors like Truly Skin show 4.8 stars with 26 reviews. More reviews = higher prominence in Maps.

**Action:** Hit 3–5 new reviews per week (see `docs/TIER-1-ACTION-PLAN.md`). Use the `/review` link and QR code at checkout.

---

## Fix #5: Proximity Reality

You can't move the business. Aurora-based "near me" will always favor businesses *in* Aurora. But adding Aurora as an area served + strong services + more reviews will push you into the results for many Aurora searchers.
